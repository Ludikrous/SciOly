What is Science Olympiad?

Science Olympiad is a prestigious competition between high schools from across the nation. Every year, students can choose from a variety of building, research, or lab projects and prepare to compete in them. We represent our school in the regionl competition in March. To prepare for this, we also participate in various invitationals throughout the course of the year. Above all, we're a group of students that have fun learning, building, and doing!
